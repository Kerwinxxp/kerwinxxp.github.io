About

I am a Ph.D. candidate at University of North Texas. My research focuses on privacy-preserving technologies and large language models (LLMs), with particular interests in location privacy, differential privacy, and machine learning applications.

Research Interests

  • Privacy
  • Large Language Models (LLM)
  • Differential Privacy
  • Location Privacy
  • Machine Learning
